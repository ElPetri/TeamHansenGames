A fast-paced tank defense game where you protect your tank from waves of balloons.

## How to Play
- **Aim**: Move your mouse to rotate the tank.
- **Shoot**: The tank shoots automatically.
- **Upgrades**: Collect money and press `Space` to buy upgrades (Fire Rate, Multi-shot).
- **Difficulty**: Choose between Easy, Medium, and **NUCLEAR**.

## Development
This is a static HTML/JS game. No build step is required.
